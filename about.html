About Pint-Sized

Pint-Sized open mic nights bring you a vibrant range of entertainment, featuring music, comedy, poetry and more. We host monthly events at The Alpaca (Essex Road); all free to attend and free to perform!

Anyone can perform at our events; all you have to do is fill out one of our quick sign up forms! So whether you are a budding talent wanting to get on stage for the first time in front of a supportive audience, or an experienced performer looking to build your following, you will be welcome to join a Pint-Sized event!

Not a performer? Not to worry! Our audience is full of friendly people who love culture and the arts, so please head round to the venue, grab a drink at the bar, and settle in with the Pint-Sized family to see incredibly talented and fresh performances.

Pint-Sized was born in 2018, founded by two friends: Farah Shair and Roshani Abbey. The first event was at The Finborough Arms in June, and thanks to the support of their friends and the ever-growing Pint-Sized community, it grew each month! Fast-forward to 2021, Pint-Sized is restarting live events following the COVID-19 pandemic with a brand new venue: The Alpaca in Islington.

There are a lot of people to thank for Pint-Sized’s success so far, so to everyone who has helped with lending equipment, managing sound systems, performing, telling friends, coming to watch, taking photos, posting on Social media, creating the website or offering advice, we are very grateful and can’t wait to keep spreading the love with these performance nights around London!

If you are a venue who would like Pint-Sized to come to you once a month, or if you are a company looking to advertise to our performers/audience, please get in touch for more details.
